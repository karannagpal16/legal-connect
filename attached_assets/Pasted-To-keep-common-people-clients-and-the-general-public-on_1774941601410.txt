To keep "common people" (clients and the general public) on RNA Legal Connect, you need to shift the app from a "one-time utility" to a "Daily Legal Companion." The goal is to provide value that doesn't require them to have an active court case. Here are the specific modules you should ask your AI to build for the Public/Client Half of the app:

1. The "Legal Health" Checkup (Interactive)

People love knowing where they stand. Create a gamified "Risk Assessment" tool.

How it works: A simple 5-question quiz (e.g., "Is your Will updated?", "Do you have a clear Rent Agreement?", "Is your MSME registered?").

The Hook: At the end, the app gives them a "Legal Wellness Score" and a free 1-page PDF checklist of what they are missing.

Retention: They stay to improve their score.

2. "Know Your Rights" (Bite-Sized Content)

In 2026, attention spans are short. Use a "Shorts/Reels" style feed for legal awareness.

How it works: A scrollable feed of 60-second text cards or AI-generated voice snippets explaining common Delhi-specific issues (e.g., "What to do if a builder delays possession in Dwarka" or "Traffic fine rules in Subhash Nagar").

Retention: Daily notifications like "Did you know? You can't be fined for X today."

3. The Free Document "Generator" (Lead Magnet)

Offer high-volume, low-stakes templates for free.

How it works: A "DIY Document" section where they can fill in a form to generate a Rent Agreement, Promissory Note, or Legal Notice for a Defective Product.

The Hook: They get a basic watermarked version for free, but have to "Contact Rishika Nagpal & Associates" (one click) to get it vetted or notarized.

4. Real-Time Case "Tracking" (The Amazon Effect)

For existing clients, make the legal process feel like a Swiggy/Zomato order.

How it works: A visual timeline of their case. Instead of calling your office, they see:

[Step 1: Filing Done] → [Step 2: Summons Issued] → [Step 3: Evidence (In Progress)].

Retention: They check the app every time they get a notification, reducing your manual workload.

5. The "Legal SOS" Button

A prominent floating button for emergencies.

How it works: If someone is at a police station or in a sudden dispute, they hit the "SOS" button. It instantly opens a chat with your AI assistant or a junior associate.

The Hook: Peace of mind. They keep the app on their home screen just in case of an emergency.

How to add this to your AI Prompt (Day 1-2):

Add this specific instruction to your Replit/Lovable chat:

"Add a 'Client Mode' to the app. When a user logs in as a 'Guest', show them a 'Legal Wellness Dashboard'. Include a 'Document Generator' for Rent Agreements and a 'Rights Feed' that shows daily legal tips in short cards. Ensure there is a prominent 'Consult RNA' button on every screen."

The Strategy (Why this works for your ROI):

Data Collection: To get the "Legal Score" or "Free Template," they provide their email/phone. This builds your Marketing Database.

Trust Building: By the time they have a serious legal issue (Property, Divorce, Corporate), RNA Legal Connect is already the most trusted app on their phone.

Community Presence: In Subhash Nagar and Hari Nagar, you become the "Digital Lawyer" everyone recommends because your app is actually useful daily.
